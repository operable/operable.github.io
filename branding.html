Branding

When you want to talk about Operable and/or Cog, use one of our excellent logos for added pizzazz. Readers like visual pizzazz...and cute robots...and kittens.

Guidelines

Please follow the listed guidelines below so we don't have to make frowny faces at you. If you have questions or additional needs, contact us at info@operable.io

  • Only place the assets on plain backgrounds.
  • Don’t tilt, skew, or bedazzle the assets.
  • The colors we selected look great as they are, please don’t alter them.
  • Don’t crowd the logo, give it room, let it breathe. Wrench icon padding on all sides is preferable.

About Cog

We believe that DevOps is a team sport and in order to win more often, we created Cog.

Cog is ChatOps turned up to 11. Built with powerful access control means that you can collaborate around even the most sensitive tasks with confidence. We focus on extensibility and adaptability, so your team can respond quickly to the unexpected, without sacrificing visibility, automation or security. Have your cake and eat it too.
