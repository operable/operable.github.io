About

We believe that DevOps is a team sport and in order to win more often, we created Cog.

Operable is based in Durham, North Carolina, directly across the street from the Durham Athletic Park. We are focused on making the lives of engineers less stressful by building tools and experiences that increase the effectiveness of their communication, collaboration, and problem solving. This focus has lead us to build Cog and most recently, Flywheel a hosted Cog service.

What is Cog?

Cog is ChatOps turned up to 11. Built with powerful access control means that you can collaborate around even the most sensitive tasks with confidence. We focus on extensibility and adaptability, so your team can respond quickly to the unexpected, without sacrificing visibility, automation or security. Have your cake and eat it too. In addition, we've recently released Flywheel, a hosted Cog service with an management user interface. No chatbot infrastructure management for the win!Learn more about Cog and Flywheel.
