Kicking it with the Kickstarter team

Engineers were running commands on their laptops, or clicking around a web UI without a lot of transparency or context...
Aaron Suggs

Does that sound familiar? The team at Kickstarter is ahead of the ChatOps game, yet even they realized that they were lacking visibility. They felt that their processes around how they managed AWS CloudFormation could be better, so together we created a command bundle for Cog that allows them to manage CloudFormation from Slack. Crowdsourced win!
