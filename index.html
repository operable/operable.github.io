What is ChatOps

ChatOps is a approach to making the communication between DevOps teams and the things they are building / managing rich in both in collaboration conversations and collaborative action. Since we've obviously consumed the ChatOps CoolAid, as Levar Burton would say, "But hey, don't take my(our) word for it."

  • "ChatOps is an approach to communication that allows teams to collaborate and manage many aspects of their infrastructure, code, and data from the comfort and safety of a chat room."

    - Jason Hand, (DevOps Evangelist - VictorOps)

  • "...paring all the time"

    - Jesse Newland, (Ops - Github)

Why Cog?

Cog is the best answer for ChatOps and team collaboration. Built with security and extensibility in mind, Cog provides the safest way to collaboratively interact with your infrastructure and easily grow Cog's capabilities based on your team's needs.

  • Built to support your current system administration / operations workflow

  • Easy to set up and start using in chat

  • Directly supports the practice of DevOps

  • At Cog's core security and auditing are first class citizens

About Cog

We believe that DevOps is a team sport and in order to win more often, we created Cog.

Cog is ChatOps turned up to 11. Built with powerful access control means that you can collaborate around even the most sensitive tasks with confidence. We focus on extensibility and adaptability, so your team can respond quickly to the unexpected, without sacrificing visibility, automation or security. Have your cake and eat it too.
