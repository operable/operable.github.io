Easy development

Command Cog to do just about anything you would do in your CLI

If you view your chatbot as a kind of "shared command line", then commands are like the executables in your terminal. In fact, you might recognize several commands from the operable namespace above from your everyday terminal usage: echo, wc, sort, etc.

Commands, Cog, and you.

Cog runs on commands (not donuts). Here are a few example commands that are probably already part of your daily workflows.

Work through incidents collaboratively right from chat.

!pagerduty:alert
Customers complaining about the site is down? Announce this to your #support channel
!pagerduty:oncall
Who's got the pager? This will tell you and the room immediately who is oncall.
!pagerduty:ack PHNRH53
Allow users to ack an incident right from chat.
